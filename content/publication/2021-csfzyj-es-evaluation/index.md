## 摘要

本研究旨在为我国空间规划提供生态系统服务评估的新思路与补充，对完善理论体系、优化空间规划、提升人类福祉具有重要意义。研究从理论综述、方法引入和方法构建三个方面展开。首先，归纳和研究各类基础资料，概述国内外生态系统服务评估方法现状。其次，结合我国实际，归纳抽象国外最新研究成果，并分类介绍模型和工具。最后，确定方法选择的评价标准，提出不同方法的应用情景，并通过决策树模拟了方法选择过程。

## Abstract

This paper investigates ecosystem service evaluation methods for spatial planning in China, aiming to offer new ideas, complement existing technologies, and enhance theoretical frameworks, spatial planning optimization, and human well-being. The research covers three aspects: theoretical review, method introduction, and method construction. It begins by summarizing basic data and reviewing current evaluation methods globally. It then synthesizes recent international research, adapting it to China's context, and categorizes models and tools. Finally, it defines method selection criteria, proposes application scenarios for different methods, and simulates the selection process using a decision tree.
